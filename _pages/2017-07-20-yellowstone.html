I went to Yellowstone (and Grand Teton) in 2015. It's obviously hard to pick, but it may still be my favorite place I've ever gone to. There are wild animals everywhere, some of the terrain feels truly alien, and it's honestly hard to find a spot there that's not insanely beautiful.
